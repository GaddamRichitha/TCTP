Key formulas used
Total project costOne-time costs + (Monthly recurring × Duration months)
Cost per unitTotal project cost ÷ Target volume + Variable cost per unit
Derived selling priceCost per unit ÷ (1 − Target margin %)
Break-even unitsTotal fixed costs ÷ (Selling price − Variable cost per unit)
Gross margin(Selling price − Cost per unit) ÷ Selling price × 100
Payback periodTotal one-time costs ÷ Monthly net profit
Projected ROI(Target revenue − Total cost) ÷ Total cost × 100
